Why Responsible Disposal Matters

Appliances Aren't Just Trash —
They Need Proper Handling

Refrigerators, AC units, and freezers contain refrigerants like R-22 and R-134a. Releasing them improperly is a federal violation under the Clean Air Act — and harmful to Seattle's air quality. We work with certified recycling partners who recover these substances safely.

Old washers and dryers contain recoverable steel and copper. We route those to metal recyclers rather than a landfill whenever possible.

  • No refrigerant venting — fully EPA compliant
  • Metal components routed to certified recyclers
  • Working units donated to Seattle charities
  • You get a clean space and a clean conscience
